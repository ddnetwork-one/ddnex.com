Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our engagement model.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we have deep experience across Healthcare, Finance, and Logistics, our expertise in cloud-native architectures applies to any data-intensive industry. We adapt our solutions to meet specific regulatory and operational needs.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive SLA-based support and maintenance packages. This includes 24/7 monitoring, security patching, and feature updates to ensure your systems remain robust and up-to-date.

How do you handle data security?

Security is integrated into our development lifecycle (DevSecOps). We adhere to ISO 27001 standards, conduct regular penetration testing, and ensure compliance with GDPR and local data protection laws.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on complexity. A typical MVP (Minimum Viable Product) takes 8-12 weeks, while full enterprise system migrations can span 6-12 months. We provide detailed roadmaps during the discovery phase.

Security Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Commitment to Security

DDNEX is committed to maintaining the highest standards of information security. Our approach is aligned with ISO 27001 best practices to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of client data.

2. Data Encryption

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption algorithms.

3. Access Control

Access to client environments and sensitive data is strictly controlled on a need-to-know basis. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all DDNEX personnel accessing internal systems.

4. Vulnerability Management

We conduct regular vulnerability scanning and penetration testing on our infrastructure and applications. Identified issues are prioritized and remediated according to industry severity standards.

5. Incident Response

DDNEX maintains a formal incident response plan to address potential security breaches promptly. Clients will be notified of any incidents affecting their data without undue delay.
